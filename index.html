About Us

We are a passionate group of creators who descided to create a community for eveyone who wants to learn to create 3D and animation.
Since we want to have equal opportunities, every software and tool we use is free and open-source.


We use Blender and it is awesome! With it you can make art, assets for games or movies or make your very own animated movie!

Why join us?

Learning is allways better together. We have a vibrant and welcoming comunity on Discord where we can help each other, discuss and show off what we are doing.
We offer guides and free rescources that will help you improve your skills on your journey to master the world of 3D.
On our weekly livestreams on youtube, everyone can join the chat as we learn together live!
